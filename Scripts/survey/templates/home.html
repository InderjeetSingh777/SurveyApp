Who we are
to Serve the nation

We collect the data from the citizens which is analysed and presented to the ward,district,state and central Leaders ; who try to address the concerns of the citizens in the best way to bring a change in the nation by creating a better governance and democracy.




Level Hierarchy
  • Level 1 (Central Level)   : The level has central leaders.
  • Level 2 (State Level)      : This level comprises of the state leaders.
  • Level 3 (District Level)   : This level has the district managers. The district managers can view the data of the people bases in his district.
  • Level 4 (Ward Level)     : This level includes the ward managers. The ward manager is responsible for and has the complete visibility of the workers in his ward                                       and the people’s reviews received within his ward.
